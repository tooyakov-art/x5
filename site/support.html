Common questions

How do I delete my account?

In the app: tap the profile icon (top right) → scroll to Danger zone → Delete Account → confirm twice. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Why does Sign in with Apple ask for my email?

Apple lets you choose between sharing your real email or using a private relay address. Either works — X5 stores only what Apple sends us.

Is X5 free?

Version 1.0 is fully free with no ads. Future versions may add optional paid features (a Pro subscription) but the core caption generator will remain free.

Are my captions private?

Captions are generated on your device. We never see them.
