Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

What we collect

When you sign in with Apple, X5 stores the following on our authentication backend (Supabase, hosted on AWS in the United States):

  • Email address — provided by Sign in with Apple. If you choose Apple's "Hide My Email" option, only the relay address is stored.
  • Name — only if you grant it during the first Apple Sign-In.
  • User ID — an internal identifier used to associate you with your account.

What we do NOT collect

  • No tracking identifiers (IDFA, IDFV).
  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or health data.
  • No analytics, no advertising SDKs, no third-party trackers.

How we use your data

Strictly for authentication — recognising you across sessions and devices. Captions you generate are produced on your device and are not sent to our servers.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only services that process your data on our behalf are:

  • Apple — handles Sign in with Apple.
  • Supabase — stores your authentication record.

Account deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Open the X5 app → tap the profile icon → scroll to Danger zone → Delete Account → confirm.
  • Or email support@x5studio.app and we will remove your account within 7 days.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Children

X5 is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify users in the app.

Contact

Questions? Email support@x5studio.app.
