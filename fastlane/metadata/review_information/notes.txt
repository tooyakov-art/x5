X5 is a marketplace for creators (photographers, designers, video editors,
copywriters, SMM managers) in Kazakhstan and Central Asia. The app has four
main areas: Hub (open tasks from clients), Chats, Portfolio and Courses.

DEMO ACCOUNT
Use the credentials in the Apple Demo Account fields. The account has a
small portfolio, an open chat, and a sample course unlocked.

QUICK TEST FLOW
1. Sign in with Apple, Google, or the demo email + password.
2. The Home tab shows the Hub — tap any task card to view details and
   send a proposal.
3. The Chats tab shows existing conversations. Open a chat to test:
   - Send text, photos (paperclip → Photos), voice (long-press microphone)
   - Long-press a bubble → Copy
   - Top-right menu → Search, Mute, Pin, Open profile, Report, Block
   - Swipe left on a chat row → Delete; swipe right → Pin / Mute
4. The Portfolio tab shows the public profile. Tap "Edit" to add a
   portfolio entry (image + caption).
5. The Courses tab lists creator-published courses. Open a course → tap
   any lesson → reading or video plays inline.
6. Settings (gear icon on profile) → Cache, Promo notifications toggle,
   Face ID lock, Sign out, Delete account.

UGC SAFETY (Guideline 1.2)
- Every chat user can be Blocked or Reported from the chat header menu.
- Hub task authors can be Blocked from their profile.
- Account deletion in Settings is two-step and removes auth + profile.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
APNs token is registered after Sign-in. A Postgres trigger calls the
send-message-push Edge Function on new messages. To test: open the app
on a second device with a different account, send a message — first
device gets a push.

PRIVACY
Sign in with Apple is offered alongside Google and email — Guideline
4.8 satisfied. No third-party trackers, no ads. Authentication and
profile/chat storage are on Supabase.
