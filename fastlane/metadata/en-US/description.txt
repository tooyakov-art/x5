X5 helps marketers ship better posts faster with platform-aware caption templates.

Pick a topic, a tone (Friendly / Pro / Funny), and a target platform — get five ready-to-post captions optimized for Instagram, X, LinkedIn or TikTok length and style.

KEY FEATURES
• Platform-aware templates — captions sized for Instagram, X, LinkedIn and TikTok
• Three tones — Friendly, Professional, Funny
• Brand voice profile — niche, audience and brand vocabulary woven into every caption
• Caption history — your last 20 sessions, always within reach
• Character and hashtag counters on every caption
• One-tap copy and native share sheet
• Sign in with Apple — privacy-first, no email required
• Account deletion right inside the app

WHO IT'S FOR
Marketers, founders, content creators, and small business owners who post multiple times per week and want consistent voice without staring at a blank screen.

PRIVACY & TRUST
Authentication uses Sign in with Apple. Captions are generated locally on your device. No third-party tracking, no analytics, no ads.
