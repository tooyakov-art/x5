X5 connects creators, marketers and clients in one place. Find work in the Hub, message clients directly, share your portfolio, and learn from courses by other creators.

KEY FEATURES
• Hub — browse open tasks from clients, send proposals, get hired
• Chats — direct messaging with photos, voice messages and search; archive, mute, pin and report controls
• Portfolio — showcase your work with a public profile other creators can follow
• Courses — bite-size lessons from creators, with video and reading material
• Profile — public or private, verified badge available, multiple languages (Russian, English, Kazakh)
• Sign in with Apple, Google or email — pick the one you trust

WHO IT'S FOR
Photographers, designers, video editors, copywriters, SMM managers and other creators in Kazakhstan and Central Asia looking for clients, peers and skills.

SAFETY
Block, report and account deletion are one tap away. Chats hide muted and archived conversations. Sign-out wipes all locally cached chat media on the device.

PRIVACY
Authentication uses Sign in with Apple, Google or email. Profile data and messages live on Supabase under Row Level Security policies. No third-party trackers, no ads.
